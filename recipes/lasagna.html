Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layered meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and lasagna noodles. There are a multitude of variations of lasagna that use ground beef, sausage, cottage cheese, etc. It can be prepared and assembled in a variety of ways and baked using countless different pans and methods. For this specific recipe we will be using and tomato based pasta sauce made with a mixture of Italian sausage ground beef. Ricotta cheese and mozzarella are called for in this recipe but the ricotta can be substituted with cottage cheese, and the mozzarella can be substituted with any preffered shredded cheese. This partucular recipe will be kept fairly simple but feel free to get creative and add extras like spinach layered in, onions and garlic when cooking the ground beef, etc,.

Have fun and enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles; uncooked
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt; more if needed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Tomato sauce
    • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
    • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
    • 1 diced yellow onion
    • 2 minced garlic cloves
    • 36 ounces tomato sauce
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Cheese mixture
    • 2 cups ricotta cheese
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 1 large egg; beaten

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add all 12 lasagna noodles and cook until firm. When noodles are done; drain hot water, rinse under cold eater, and set to the side.
  3. In large skillet over a medium-high heat, brown the beef, sausage, onion, and garlic. Drain fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Add additional salt and pepper if needed.
  5. In a separate medium bowl, compine 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, egg, and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  6. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a 9x13 casserole dish or any simlilar sized casserole dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer nooodles with 1/3 of the chhese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice and finish the last layer with the remaining sauce.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and sprinkle with the remaining 2 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until brown on top. Broil 2-3 minutes if desired.
  10. Let rest for at least 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
